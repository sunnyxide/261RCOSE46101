## Specific anti-Western failure modes

- **삼성/LG 충성도** — Korean appliance loyalty is family-level multi-generational
  ("우리 집은 삼성") in ways that defy Western brand-loyalty Likert scoring
- **효도 가전** — Adult children buying premium electronics for parents
  (안마의자, 공기청정기, 김치냉장고) — distinct consumer ≠ user ≠ payer triangle
- **다나와 가격비교 → 단톡방** — Korean tech buyers do quantitative price
  research first (다나와) then social validation (단톡방) — Western reverse
- **디시·뽐뿌·클리앙** — Korean tech community recommendations carry
  evidence-weight that overlaps but differs from Western Reddit/HN
